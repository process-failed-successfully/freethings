Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All tools on FreeThings.win operate entirely within your browser. Your files, text, and generated content never leave your device.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

FreeThings.win is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not:

  • Collect or store any files you upload or process
  • Track your usage patterns or behavior
  • Require user accounts, registrations, or login credentials
  • Store any generated content (QR codes, passwords, converted files, etc.)
  • Collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Use analytics tracking that identifies individual users

2. How Our Tools Work

All processing on FreeThings.win happens client-side in your web browser:

  • QR Code Generator: QR codes are generated in your browser using JavaScript
  • Image Tools: Image conversion and resizing happens locally using Canvas API
  • Text Tools: All text processing occurs in your browser's memory
  • Password Generator: Passwords are generated using your browser's cryptographic functions
  • Unit Converter: All calculations are performed locally

None of your data is sent to our servers or any third-party services for processing.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may collect and use information about your visit to this and other websites through the use of cookies and similar technologies.

What Google AdSense Collects:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited on our site
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Referring website

How to Control Ad Personalization:

  • Visit Google Ads Settings to control ad personalization
  • Opt out of personalized advertising at aboutads.info
  • Learn more in Google's Advertising Privacy Policy

3.2 Content Delivery Networks (CDNs)

We use the following CDNs to deliver fonts, icons, and libraries:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (Inter font family)
  • Cloudflare CDN: For Font Awesome icons and JavaScript libraries

These services may collect limited technical information such as IP addresses for security and performance purposes. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy

4. Cookies

Our website does not set any first-party cookies for tracking purposes. However, third-party services may use cookies:

Third-Party Cookies

  • Google AdSense: Uses cookies to serve relevant advertisements and measure ad performance
  • DoubleClick: Google's advertising service may use cookies for ad serving

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser

Note: Blocking cookies may affect the functionality of advertisements but will not impact the core functionality of our tools.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your personal data, there is minimal risk of data breaches related to your tool usage. However, we implement security best practices:

  • HTTPS: All connections are encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Security Headers: We implement CSP, X-Frame-Options, and other security headers
  • No Server-Side Storage: Your files and data never touch our servers
  • Client-Side Processing: All operations occur in your browser's secure sandbox

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not:

  • Target content to children under 13
  • Use interest-based advertising targeting children
  • Collect personal information from users we know to be under 13

7. International Data Transfers

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, which may serve content from servers located worldwide. While we do not collect your personal data, third-party services like Google AdSense may transfer data internationally. These services comply with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR.

8. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Although we do not collect personal data ourselves, you have rights regarding data collected by third-party services:

European Union (GDPR) Rights:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data collected about you
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing for advertising
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how your data is used

California (CCPA) Rights:

  • Right to Know: Know what personal information is collected
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of personal information

To exercise these rights regarding Google AdSense data, visit:

  • Google Account Privacy Settings
  • Google Ad Personalization Controls

9. Do Not Track (DNT)

We respect Do Not Track browser settings. However, note that third-party advertising services may not honor DNT signals. We recommend using browser extensions or privacy tools to block tracking if this is a concern.
