Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the conversions?

Our conversions use standard conversion factors and are accurate to the precision displayed. For critical applications, always verify with authoritative sources.

Can I convert between any units?

You can convert between any units within the same category. For example, you can convert between any length units, but not between length and weight.

What's the difference between US and Imperial units?

US customary units and Imperial units differ for some measurements (like gallons and fluid ounces). Our converter uses US customary units by default.

Can I copy the results?

Yes! Click the "Copy Result" button to copy the converted value to your clipboard for easy pasting into other applications.

Are there any limitations?

Our converter handles all standard unit conversions. For very large or very small numbers, scientific notation may be used for clarity.

Is this converter free to use?

Yes! Our unit converter is completely free to use with no limitations, watermarks, or hidden costs.
