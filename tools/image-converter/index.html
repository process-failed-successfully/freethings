Frequently Asked Questions

How does SVG to JPG conversion handle transparency?

When converting SVG to JPG, transparent areas are automatically filled with a background color (default white). You can choose any background color or convert to PNG to preserve transparency.

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including SVG, JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, and TIFF. You can convert between any of these formats.

Is there a file size limit?

File size is limited by your browser's memory. Very large images (over 50MB) may cause performance issues, but most common images work perfectly.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

Yes! You can upload multiple images and convert them all with the same settings. Download them individually or all at once.

Are my images secure?

Yes! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Images are never uploaded to our servers or stored anywhere.
